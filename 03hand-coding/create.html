Create & Express

🎥 Start a Mini Vlog Series
Document your summer adventures by recording and editing short videos to share with friends or post online.

🎨 Host a Paint & Pizza Night
Gather your friends for a laid-back evening of painting canvases and sharing your favorite pizza.

👚 DIY Thrift Flips
Buy secondhand clothes and transform them into fresh, one-of-a-kind pieces with sewing, dyeing, or embellishments.

📷 Summer Photo Challenge
Set daily or weekly photography prompts to inspire you to see the world in new ways.

🎵 Design a Summer Playlist
Curate a collection of songs that capture the mood of your summer and share it with friends.

📝 Create a Vision Board
Collect images, quotes, and goals that inspire you, then arrange them on a board to visualize your dreams.

🍹 Try Trendy Recipes
Experiment in the kitchen by making popular treats like homemade boba, fancy mocktails, or aesthetic desserts.

📖 Make a Scrapbook or Polaroid Wall
Print photos and keepsakes to build a physical record of your favorite summer memories.
